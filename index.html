CV playbook (neon‑clean)

  1. Header: role · location/time‑zone · links.
  2. Impact bullets: Metric → Mechanism → Meaning. Lead with outcomes.
  3. Stack: list core tools; avoid buzzword clouds.
  4. Projects: 3–5 wins with numbers; link proof.
  5. Keep it skimmable: 1–2 pages, active voice, consistent tense.
